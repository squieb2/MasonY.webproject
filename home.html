Welcome to Game Log!

Game Log is a website that allows users of all platforms to log the games they play, the amount of hours poured into a game, and rate games. At Game Log, our platform is meticulously crafted with a diverse and vibrant gaming community in mind. Our target audience encompasses a wide spectrum of gaming enthusiasts, fostering an inclusive environment that welcomes individuals of all ages, genders, and educational backgrounds.

Game Log's listed games link to the steam page to give you more information. Furthermore, the data determining the amount of players playing a game currently are courtesy of SteamDB!

Game Log's purpose

The purpose of this site is to let users view games based on when the game released, how popular the game currently is, genres, and store games to a table that lets the user rate the game on a scale of 1-10. The functionality for adding games to a list and rating has sadly not been implemented. Stay tuned!

Target Audience

Game Log's target audience is for anyone who enjoys playing games and wants a system to rate the games they have played or completed.

For Gaming Enthusiasts

If you are passionate about gaming, love exploring new titles, and find joy in sharing your experiences and opinions, Game Log is made for you. Our platform provides a space for you to log the the games you play, track your play time, and rate the games you played!

For Any ages between 13-50

This site is catered to any individuals who are at the minimum age of 13 offerinbg a shared space for teenagers, young adults, and everyone in between. No matter your age, if you love gaming, you're right at home here.

Inlcusivity for All Genders

We welcome individuals of all genders. Our community is built on the principle that gaming is for any and everyone.

Education Level & Steam Users

Game Log is open to individuals of any education level. Whether you're a high school student, or a college graduate, our plat form connects individuals who share a passion for gaming. Game Log is geared mainly towards Steam users since the games in each category lead to the steam page, giving you more information about the game.
